Date: Summer 2020
Medium: Graphite
A drawing meticulously crafted with reference images and careful consideration of details. The pine tree, stone temple, and sparrows in the scene are meant to make it look like a normal, traditionally styled Japanese garden, but the lilypad path going through the torii gate makes it look as if it could be a pathway to the heavens.
