About Nanako Keney

I am a high school student whose hobbies include playing the piano, reading, writing, and of course, drawing and animating. Recently, I discovered that I enjoy creating HTML websites as well.

Most of my art skills are self-taguht, but I did attend art lessons for one year at a studio where I learned techniques for drawing with graphite and watercolor. Drawing programs I have used in the past include Krita, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Scratch. Although I have experience with drawing realistically, my typical artstyle is a lot closer to Japanese mangas or animes. A lot of my art is also influenced by Japanese culture or mythology.
