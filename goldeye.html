Date: December 2020
Mediums: Prisma Color Colored Pencils, Sharpie, Graphite
A drawing of a cat's eye surrounded with rosette markings. Though the markings aren't placed in realistic positions, their shape is meant to mirror the rosettes found on some housecats. I chose to color the outline and pupil with sharpie to create contrast and make the colored iris of the eye pop even more. The sharpie sections also contrast with the softer graphite of the fur.
