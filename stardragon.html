Date: Spring 2018
Mediums: Colored Pencil, Black Pen
A dragon from the Milky Way accompanied by the ring of bells and the cold light of the stars. The strings and bells present throughout the image are loosely based off the bells used by Japanese miko. It's fins and blueish colors are because its alternate form is based off of the two fish constellation pisces.
